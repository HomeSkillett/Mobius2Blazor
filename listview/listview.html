ListView / ListBox

Scrollable selection lists used throughout Mobius for master lists, settings panels, vehicle lists, and anywhere users pick from a visible collection. Distinct from ItemsControl (non-selectable repeater) and ComboBox (dropdown selector).

ListBox (Default)

Vertical scrollable list with right-edge border. Items show a left selection bar on select, hover overlay at 30% opacity. Min-height 44px (touch target). .mob-listbox + .mob-listbox__item

ListView

Full-row selection fill (vs ListBox's left-bar-only selection). Supports drag-reorder in WPF. Right-edge border like ListBox. .mob-listview + .mob-listview__item
