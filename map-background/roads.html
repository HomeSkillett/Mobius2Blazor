Road Types with Direction Arrows

Roads are drawn as filled polygons with outline, center lines, edge fills, and direction arrows (chevrons). Chevrons indicate lane direction and road type. Arrows render when zoom Scale ≤ ArrowThreshold (default 0.5). Road type is determined by NumLanes and BidirectionalDriving properties on IRoadShape. Chevrons are 3-point polylines (V shapes) drawn with LaneCenterBrush at 0.25 world-unit width. Arrow length = min(1.5, laneWidth × 0.5). Spacing = laneWidth × e (~2.718).
