Path Assignment States

When a path is assigned to a vehicle, it renders in one of four assignment states based on the AssignmentStatus enum (IObjective.cs). Each state has outline, fill, and connection colors with selected/not-selected variants.

Assignment State: Planning

AssignmentStatus.Planning — path is being computed by the planner. Always renders at full opacity (no selected/not-selected variant).

Road Shapes

Roads are drawn as filled polygons with outline, center lines, edge fills, and direction arrows (chevrons). Chevrons indicate lane direction and road type. Arrows render when zoom Scale ≤ ArrowThreshold (default 0.5). Source: RoadShapeControl.cs line 266, RoadSettingsProvider.cs line 21

Road Types with Direction Arrows

Road type is determined by NumLanes and BidirectionalDriving properties on IRoadShape. Chevrons are 3-point polylines (V shapes) drawn with LaneCenterBrush at 0.25 world-unit width. Arrow length = min(1.5, laneWidth × 0.5). Spacing = laneWidth × e (~2.718). Source: RoadUIUtilities.cs lines 13, 58, 97-120
