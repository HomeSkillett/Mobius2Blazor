Map Shape Types

Common Shapes

Core shape types from MapContentControl.xaml. Available in every Mobius configuration.

Driveable Area
Navigation polygon. Defines where autonomous vehicles are allowed to drive.
--DriveableShapeOutline / Fill
Reference [TEXTURED]
Visual-only boundary. Non-drivable reference area shown for operator context.
Zone checkerboard + dashed [2,2]
Transparent Reference
Invisible planning boundary. Used for route calculations without rendering on the map.
Transparent outline, no fill
Obstacle [TEXTURED]
Static collision zone. Physical barrier that blocks all vehicle paths. Not selectable at runtime.
Diagonal stripes (45deg)
Obstacle MapBuilder [TEXTURED]
Editable obstacle. Same collision rules as Obstacle, but selectable for editing in MapBuilder.
Diagonal stripes (red, 45deg)
Road Shape
Structured lane corridor with lanes, widths, and driving direction for vehicle routing.
--RoadShapeOutline / Fill
Intersection
Auto-generated where roads overlap. Inherits speed limit from connected roads.
--IntersectionShapeOutline / Fill
Failure Shape
Validation error state. Highlights shapes that fail MapBuilder checks (e.g., roads too close).
--FailureShapeOutline / Fill
AOZ (Area of Operations)
Operational boundary. Defines the outer perimeter vehicles must stay within.
3-layer: bg(6) + solid(4) + dashed(4)
Sensed Obstacle
Live-detected obstacle from vehicle sensors. Appears at runtime, never selectable, auto-clears when resolved.
Point marker: ellipse + dashed ring
+ EStop icon ()

Shared Plugin Shapes

From Plugins/Common/. Available across all plugin configurations (Station, Barrier, Avoid, Lockout).

Station [TEXTURED]
Named destination. Where vehicles park, charge, refuel, or perform specific actions. Spot point decoration shows when zoomed in.
Zone checkerboard (orange)
+ SpotPoint (QuePoint* tokens)
Avoid Zone [TEXTURED]
Soft exclusion area. Planner reroutes vehicles around this zone when possible.
Vertical stripes (tan)
SoftObstacle pattern
Barrier [TEXTURED]
Hard obstacle placed manually in MapBuilder. Blocks all vehicle paths with full collision mask.
Cross-hatch (gray)
HardObstacle pattern
MapBuilder Barrier [TEXTURED]
Selectable barrier variant for MapBuilder editing. Red stripes indicate editable state.
Diagonal stripes (red, 45deg)
Queue Point
Decoration overlay on queueable shapes. Shows vehicle queue position and heading. Only visible when zoomed in.
QuePoint* tokens (green)
40x40 grid, 10x10 inner dot
Safety Locked (Warning)
Shape locked by another user or system. Obstacle-stripe overlay + dashed orange boundary + lock icon.
Obstacle pattern (orange)
CriticalState=false
Safety Locked (Critical)
Critical safety lock. Same structure as warning, but InformationRed signals higher severity.
Obstacle pattern (red)
CriticalState=true

Agriculture — Golf / Mowing

From Plugins/Agriculture/Mowing/GolfCourseShapes/. Golf-course-specific shape types with inner obstacle buffers.

Green
Golf green boundary. Inner obstacle buffer protects putting surfaces from mowers.
--GreenShapeOutline / Fill
Rough [TEXTURED]
Golf rough mowing zone. Drivable area that requires a final cleanup pass.
Zone checkerboard (2-layer)
Water Hazard [TEXTURED]
Golf water boundary. Keeps mowers away from ponds and streams.
Cross-hatch (45deg, blue)
Bunker [TEXTURED]
Golf sand trap. Inner obstacle boundary keeps mowers out of bunker areas.
Scattered dots (sand)
KeepOut [TEXTURED]
Absolute no-go zone. Vehicles must never enter this area.
Cross-hatch (red, 2-layer)

Agriculture — Common

From Plugins/Agriculture/Common/. Shared across all agriculture configurations (mowing, farming, etc.).

Coverage Area [TEXTURED]
Systematic mowing/work zone. Defines an area the vehicle must cover completely.
Vertical stripes (green)
SoftObstacle pattern
Coverage History
Already-covered area within a Coverage Area. Shows completed work as a faint gray overlay.
--AreaCoverageProgressShape*
Flat fill, no pattern
Disengage [TEXTURED]
Tool-off transit zone. Vehicles can cross but must deactivate their implement.
Vertical stripes (blue)
SoftObstacle pattern
Negative Obstacle [TEXTURED]
Terrain depression. Marks ditches or drops invisible to standard sensors.
Scattered dots (red on blue)

Construction

From Plugins/Construction/. Shapes for autonomous loading, pile placement, and solar panel installation.

Autonomous Load [TEXTURED]
Vehicle loading zone. Zone checkerboard fill with yellow/gray caution-stripe border.
Zone checkerboard (sand)
+ caution border (yellow/gray)

Material pile boundary for autonomous loaders. White halo + green outline over vertical stripe fill.
Vertical stripes (green)
+ white halo (6px) + green stroke (2px)

Mining

Load Shape [TEXTURED]
Mining loading zone. Where haul trucks are loaded by excavators. Yellow/gray caution border marks the active area.
Zone checkerboard (sand)
+ caution border (yellow/gray)
Paddock Dump [TEXTURED]
Open dump area for haul trucks. Flat paddock zone where material is deposited.
Zone checkerboard (sand)
Outline only, no caution border
Edge Dump [TEXTURED]
Dump zone at a pit edge. Amber berm edge line marks the tip-head boundary.
Zone checkerboard (sand)
+ berm edge (amber, 5px)
Crusher Dump [TEXTURED]
Dump zone at a crusher hopper. Amber edge lines mark the crusher feed boundaries (1-2 edges).
Zone checkerboard (sand)
+ crusher edges (amber, 4m)

Shape States

Selection, locked, and filtered-out states applied to shapes.

Default
Normal rendering. Shape shows its base fill and outline.
Selected
Active selection. Highlight ring drawn behind the shape (bg + fg layers).
Locked
Editing locked by another user. Dashed orange outline, no lock icon.
Filtered Out (0.175)
Hidden by active layer filter. Reduced to 17.5% opacity.

AOZ Shape — Layer Breakdown

Three stacked strokes: MapBackground bg (6px) > DriveableShapeFill solid (4px) > DriveableShapeOutline dashed [1,1] (4px)

Layer 1: Background (6px)
Layer 2: + Solid (4px)
Layer 3: + Dashed [1,1] (4px)
