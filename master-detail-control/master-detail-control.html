MasterDetailControl

Default — Item Selected (HasItems)

Two-column layout: a fixed-width master list on the left (--panel-master-list-width: 236px) with a flexible detail pane on the right. The left column has an EdgeBorderVertical (right border line). The list uses SortableGroupListView with single-selection and virtualisation. Selected item highlights with SelectionPrimary background.

No Selection (HasItemsNoneSelected)

When the list has items but nothing is selected, the detail pane shows the NoSelectedItemContent placeholder. The delete bar remains visible but logically disabled (no item to act on). VisualState: HasItemsNoneSelected.

Static Variant — Grouped with Advanced Toggle

StaticMasterDetailControl uses a CollectionViewSource with GroupDescriptions to cluster items. Each group wraps in an ExpanderItem. A ToggleButton at the bottom shows/hides advanced items. The detail pane binds to SelectedItem.DetailVisualElement.
